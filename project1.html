About


Things become less ephemeral when supported by Matter, that which we can feel with all of our senses. A Matter of Growth is a conception of data into the physical world.

Functions and numbers are hidden in everything we see. Our eyes alone are not capable to perceive the underlaying patterns of nature.

It is Science purpose to decoded our surroundings; mathematics being the language we use to write our observations of the world around us.

This is not a recreation of a known shape in nature.
This is not an interpretation of a living form.
This is an attempt to reveal that which we cannot see, to unearth what is behind a Matter.
This is meant to show how beautiful the language of math could be.
#It is all made by patterns, and we are getting closer to revealing them.
This is an interpretation of How Matter is made.

Data modelling of a series of hexagonal panels made of gypsum with a focus on room acoustic performances.

Consider the data designed as a DNA. The set of algorithms inheret to it model and sculpt the final Matter through a chained reproduction of data points.

Algorithms-aided design tools make it possible to take this DNA and, by changing its values, be able to conceive n numbers of combinations for a new shape to grow and expand.

Each module created from this DNA will be able to adapt to each other and together evolve into a new form in Nature.
